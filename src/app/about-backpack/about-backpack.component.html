About Backpack

Backpack is a responsive homework organizer designed for elementary school students.

Our application combines the proven research behind the Pomodoro Technique™ and Kanban Method™ paired with a real time “Report Card” to help kids build the valuable life skills of time organization, task management, and the ability to focus.

The Backpack experience begins by guiding children to add “Learning Adventures” to their day. Once they are ready, they are guided to the “Adventure Board” where they can see what Adventures they have and what progress they have made. The handy timer keeps track of their time, so they learn when to give their brain a break. When the student finishes their day, Backpack offers confetti and congratulations for a job well done and encourages them to come back tomorrow.

With Backpack, kids will take the valuable life skills of organizing, focusing, and finishing into their next exciting Adventure.
